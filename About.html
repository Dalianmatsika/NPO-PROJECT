Mission & Vision

**Mission:** To empower the local community by providing free access to essential services in education, employment, and mental health support, fostering resilience and independence.

**Vision:** To build a self-sustaining community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, free from the barriers of poverty and limited resources.
