Our Programs

Explore our diverse range of services designed to support our community members.

After-School Tutoring

Provides academic support for students of all ages. We offer help with homework, test preparation, and key subject areas.

Job Placement Assistance

Connects job seekers with local employers, provides resume writing workshops, and offers interview coaching.

Mental Health Support

Offers free counseling sessions and support groups to promote emotional well-being and resilience within the community.

Community Workshops

From financial literacy to parenting skills, our workshops are designed to equip community members with practical life skills.
