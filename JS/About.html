About Our Organisation

Founded in 2010 by residents, Helping Hand has grown from a small volunteer-run food bank into a multi-faceted non-profit organisation. We offer a wide range of programs, including after-school tutoring, job placement assistance, mental health support, and community workshops.

Our Team

We are a small but passionate team of staff and volunteers dedicated to serving our community. Our leadership is comprised of long-time residents and experts in social work, education, and community development.
