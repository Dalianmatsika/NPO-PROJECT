About The Helping Hand Community

Founded in 2010, The Helping Hand Community Centre has been a cornerstone of support for our local residents. What started as a small, volunteer-run food bank has grown into a multi-faceted non-profit organization dedicated to fostering a more connected and thriving community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible resources, foster personal growth, and create a supportive environment for all. We believe that a strong community is built on compassion, mutual support, and the empowerment of every individual. Our programs are designed to meet the immediate needs of our neighbors while also providing the tools they need to build a better future for themselves and their families.
