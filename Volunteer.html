Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering at The Helping Hand Community is a rewarding way to directly contribute to the success and well-being of local residents. We rely on passionate individuals like you to run our programs effectively.

Current Opportunities:

  • After-School Tutors (Math, Science, English)
  • Mentors for Job Placement Assistance
  • Event Support & Fundraising Staff
  • Administrative Support (Filing, Data Entry)
